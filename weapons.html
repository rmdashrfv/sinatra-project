Final Fantasy Tactics Weapons

Much like other items in the game, Final Fantasy Tactics weapons are mostly available for purchase in shops. However, there are three other ways to acquire weapons (especially rare ones): you can find them on the battlefield using the Move-Find Item movement ability, poach them from the pelts of monsters, or acquire them by theft.

The Final Fantasy Tactics weapons list below shows each individual weapon and downloadable icon, followed by its weapon power, physical evade, location, cost, and attributes.
